How to Use the Progress Tracker

  • Track Your Progress: Select the month and enter your fitness progress percentage. This helps you track changes over time, ensuring you stay on course with your goals.
  • Visualize Your Progress: After entering your data, the chart will display a visual representation of your progress, highlighting trends and helping you identify areas for improvement.
  • Save Your Data: Once you've entered your progress for the month, click "Save Progress" to store the data. This allows you to reference it in the future and track long-term improvements.
