Customize your workout routine with a variety of exercises and sets. Track your fitness journey with ease.

How to Use the Workout Planner

  • Choose Workout Type: Select the type of workout (Cardio, Strength, Flexibility) from the dropdown menu.
  • Enter Exercise: Type the name of the exercise you'd like to add to your workout routine in the input field.
  • Save Workout: After selecting your workout type and entering an exercise, click the "Save Workout" button to add it to your workout list.
  • View Saved Workouts: Your saved workouts will be displayed in a list below. You can track your exercises and ensure your routine stays varied.
